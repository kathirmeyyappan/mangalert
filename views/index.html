Welcome to MangAlert!

What is MangAlert?

The goal of this service is to periodically notify the user (by email) whenever an anime/manga on their MyAnimeList plan-to-read or plan-to-watch list has finished airing/serializing. The user (once logged in) can also view all of their planned entries which have ended. If you like to binge your media, this is for you!
